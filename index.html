How WellValet works

Four simple steps to smarter, healthier shopping. Your valet takes care of the rest.

1
Brief Your Valet

Enter your age, weight, health goals, known conditions and allergies once. Your valet remembers everything.

2
Scan at the Shelf

Point your camera at any barcode in the supermarket for an instant personalised result.

3
Get Your Score

Receive a personalised health score: Good, Moderate or Avoid — with full explanation and better alternatives.

Live Better

Use your meal planner and purchase history to build healthier habits — week by week, your valet guides the way.
