Your wellness valet — always on duty

From the supermarket shelf to your bathroom cabinet, WellValet guides every wellness decision — food, beauty and family — with intelligence and care.

📷
Instant Barcode Scan

Point your camera at any product and get an instant wellness score in seconds. No typing, no searching — food or beauty.

💄
Beauty Product Scanning

Scan skincare, shampoo, lotions and cosmetics. Parabens, sulfates and harmful chemicals flagged instantly with a skin wellness score.

🔍
OCR Ingredient Scanner

Point your camera at any ingredients label. WellValet reads and analyses every ingredient using AI — even for unlisted products.

🎯
Personalised Scores

Wellness scores tailored to your age, weight, goals, known conditions and allergies. Because your valet knows you personally.

⚠️
Allergen Alerts

Instantly flags allergens in product ingredients based on your personal allergy profile. Your valet always has your back.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Family Shopping List

Share a live shopping list with up to 4 family members. See exactly who added what — Mike: Cheese, Sophie: Milk, Cornflakes.

🍽️
Weekly Meal Planner

Dietitian-inspired meal plans generated from your purchase history. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks — planned for you.

🛒
Smart Shopping List

Build and manage your shopping list directly in the app. Never forget what you need — your valet remembers everything.

📋
Purchase History

Track every product you have bagged. Filter by Good, Moderate or Care to understand your buying patterns over time.
